How To Help Yourself

Taking time to care for your mental and physical wellbeing is one of the best things you can do for yourself. Taking a break every now and then to go outside, exercise, or play some video games is sometimes the perfect way to restore some drive to your brain.


Some things you could do:

  • Hike
  • Nap
  • Meditate
  • Watch TV
